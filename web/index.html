✦ About Vibhuti Yoga ✦

"Vibhuti Yoga" — the "Yoga of Divine Manifestations" — is the name given to the tenth chapter of the Bhagavad Gita. In this sacred chapter, Lord Krishna reveals His divine glories, omnipresence, and universal form to Arjuna, emphasizing that He is the source and essence of all beings and things.

🕉️ Understanding the Name

Vibhuti (విభూతి) — means wealth, opulence, or greatness. Here it refers to the divinity, powers, and universal form of God.

Yoga (యోగ) — means a path or practice. Here it refers to the path of connection with God.

Vibhuti Yoga — The tenth chapter of the Bhagavad Gita, in which Krishna reveals His Vibhutis — divine powers and greatness — to Arjuna. It describes how Krishna's divine energy manifests in the universe's most powerful, beautiful, and sacred aspects.

✨ Key Teachings

God is the source of all virtues, knowledge, and emotions. The chapter encourages deep devotion by recognizing His presence everywhere and offering thoughts and actions with love and remembrance.

Understanding Vibhuti Yoga helps devotees transcend material illusion by realizing the Divine is present in everything — transforming everyday life into a sacred experience.
