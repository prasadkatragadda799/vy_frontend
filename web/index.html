🕉️ What Vibhuti Yoga Means

Vibhuti Yoga — also known as the "Yoga of Divine Manifestations" — is the name given to the tenth chapter of the Bhagavad Gita. In this sacred chapter, Lord Krishna reveals His divine glories and omnipresence to Arjuna, proclaiming that He is the source of all beings and all things. Every expression of brilliance, strength, and beauty in this universe is a spark of His infinite splendour.

✦ Vibhuti — Divine Glory & Supreme Power

Vibhuti means wealth, opulence, and greatness. It refers to the divinity, extraordinary powers, and universal form of the Supreme Consciousness. Vibhuti is also the holy ash — Bhasma — worn by seekers as a mark of purification and surrender. Just as fire transforms matter into its purest essence, Vibhuti represents the soul's journey from the material to the divine — a reminder that beneath every form lies something indestructible and luminous.

✦ Yoga — The Path of Union

Yoga is far more than physical practice. It is the ancient science of union — connecting the individual self (Jeevatma) with the Universal Self (Paramatma). Through disciplined practice, meditation, and self-awareness, Yoga awakens the dormant faculties within every human being and transforms everyday life into a sacred experience.

✦ The Divine Manifestations

In the tenth chapter of the Bhagavad Gita, Lord Krishna reveals how His divine energy manifests in the universe's most powerful, beautiful, and sacred aspects. He is the origin and essence of everything — from the elements of nature to the most revered beings. Understanding Vibhuti Yoga helps devotees transcend material illusion by realising that the Divine is present in all things.

✦ Awakening the 6th & 7th Sense

Vibhuti Yoga is the path of awakening the extraordinary powers that already reside within us — the miraculous faculties of the 6th and 7th senses. It is a Rishi-inspired programme that draws upon ancient Vedic wisdom to unlock intuition, sharpen intellect, enhance memory, and awaken the legendary 'Third Eye' — enabling perception far beyond the ordinary.
